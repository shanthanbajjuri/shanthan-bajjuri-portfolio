About Me

I'm Shanthan Bajuri, an engineer with a background in Electronics and Communication from Chaitanya Institute of Technology and Science. My passion for both hardware and software led me to pursue a Master’s in Computer Science at Anderson University, South Carolina—where I’m currently expanding my expertise in intelligent systems and AI.

I thrive on solving complex problems and building devices that interact with the real world. My interests span embedded systems, wearable technology, and sensor-driven automation. I’m deeply fascinated by how low-level programming and data analytics can come together to create smart, responsive solutions.

My technical toolkit includes the basics of C++, Python, R, ONNX Runtime, and foundational knowledge in AWS cloud security and cybersecurity. I enjoy experimenting with AI models and integrating them into hardware environments to push the boundaries of what’s possible.

I’m actively seeking internship and full-time opportunities where I can gain hands-on experience, contribute to innovative hardware-software integration, and continue growing as a builder of intelligent systems.
